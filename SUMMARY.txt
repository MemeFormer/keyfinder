🔧 TECHNICAL SPECS:

Key Detection:
  • Algorithm: Krumhansl-Schmuckler
  • FFT: 16384 samples
  • Hop size: 4096 (75% overlap)
  • Frequency range: 80-4000 Hz
  • Weighting: Harmonic (bass priority)
  • Output: 24 keys + Camelot notation

BPM Detection:
  • Method: Onset strength + autocorrelation
  • FFT: 2048 samples
  • Hop size: 512
  • Range: 60-180 BPM
  • Precision: 0.1 BPM

Supported Formats:
  MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, AIFF
